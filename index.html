About me

My path to web development, and beyond!

My name is Amaar Esufally, and my journey to web development has been a unique one. I graduated from the London School of Economics in 2016 with a 2:1 Honors and a BSc in Management. After graduating, I interned at the Sri Lankan Permanent Mission to the United Nations. It was during this time that I discovered my passion for coding, started self studying, and went through App Academy in New York City.

After getting a grounding in web development, and working on a few fun projects, I joined Sri Lankan telehealth startup oDoc as a Product and Operations manager. After a few months, I took the lead on product, and have been working on product ever since!
